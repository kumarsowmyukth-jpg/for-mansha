Mansha 🌸

Today is not just your birthday.

It is the day your soul chose Krishna.
The day you turned toward something eternal.

And that devotion in you…
It is rare.
May Krishna bless you deeply.

May His flute guide your heart.
May His presence protect your path.
May His love surround you always.

You are seen.
You are protected.
You are cherished by Him.
Whether or not I stand beside you one day,
I truly wish you grow higher in spirituality.

May your bhakti deepen.
May your connection with Krishna become unshakable.
May you always feel His love within you.
There is something I wrote for you long ago…

I want to give everything to the Lord
while you being by my side.

The only proof of this wish is Krishna,
who watches everything from my heart.

He knows how much I want this…
and still, He teaches me patience.
I cannot wait to visit Vrindavan again.
I cannot wait to watch us dance.
I cannot wait to talk about Krishna for hours and hours.

Just to serve our Lord Krishna till the end of our life together.

That is all I need.
That is all I ever wanted.
But even if destiny writes something different…

My prayer for you will remain.
My respect for you will remain.
My sincerity will remain.

No pressure.
No force.
Only truth.

Mansha 🌸

Under Krishna’s sky, everything unfolds in divine timing.

Jaggu loves you.
And so does Somi.

Radhe Krishna.
