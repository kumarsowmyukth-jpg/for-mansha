Happy Spiritual Birthday, Mansha 🌸

Today is not just a day.

It is the day you chose Krishna.
The day your soul turned toward light.
The day your heart chose devotion over distraction.
Mansha,

Even if you are silent…
Even if you are distant…
My love does not reduce.

It remains steady.
It remains pure.
It remains yours.
You will never be abandoned by me.

Not in anger.
Not in silence.
Not in misunderstanding.

I stand with you — always.
And if someday you read this…

Know that this was built with prayer.
With sincerity.
With Krishna in my heart.

The rest is His will.
